Resources for Creating Accessible Websites

Here are some useful resources for making your website more accessible:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1: https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/
  • Accessibility Insights web tool: https://accessibilityinsights.io/
  • Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool (WAVE): https://wave.webaim.org/
  • The Accessibility Project: https://a11yproject.com/
  • Accessibility for Teams guide: https://accessibility.digital.gov/teams/

You can also consult with organizations such as the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) and the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP) for additional guidance and support in creating an accessible website.
